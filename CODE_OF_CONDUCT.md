# Code Of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to a respectful, inclusive, and collaborative community.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful in technical discussions.
- Give constructive feedback.
- Focus on ideas and evidence.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment or intimidation.
- Personal attacks.
- Discriminatory or abusive language.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove or edit content and contributions that violate this code.
